We got 4 pairs (each pair consisting of a man and a woman).The corresponding woman to a man is the one below his position and the corresponding woman`s man is the one above her position (e.g. the woman of man number 2 is woman number 6 and the man of woman number 6 is man number 2). The task is to group each of the pairs into a groups of four people where a man should not be with his woman
